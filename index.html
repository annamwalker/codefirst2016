Za'atari's Entrepreneurs

The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan is currently hosting more than a million refugees. Za'atari Refugee Camp is 12 kilometres from the Syrian border and is the largest Syrian refugee camp in the world. Rana Abdelhamid, student at the Harvard Kennedy School and founder of the Women's Initiative for Self Empowerment (WISE), spent this summer with a group of teenage girls in Za'atari. They asked us if we could help them make a photo campaign about sexual harrassment in the camp. These are their photos.
